Advanced custom forum integration

Forum integration is a pretty complicated feature and we'll only consider it for very special cases.

What can be done:

  • One click sign in with your existing forum accounts.
  • Run private boards where only your forum members are allowed.

Existing custom integrations

Pixelation forum

The kick-ass pixel artist at pixelation are the first users and beta testers of tzigla. We would like to especially thank Sven Ruthner (ptoing), who has been a great supporter of tzigla since the beginning.

Public integrations

Anyone with a Facebook or Twitter account can safely sign in into tzigla and start drawing.
