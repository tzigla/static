Couchsurfing South America

During our trip in South America, we've asked each person we've couchsurfed with to draw a tile and after about 4 months, the board was completed.

The board is an A4 sheet and has 16 tiles made by 16 artists from Brazil, Uruguay, Argentina and Chile. It was created between november 2009 and march 2010. The list of artists will be added soon.
