Street Delivery 2011

Street Delivery is an urban festival that takes the street away from cars and gives it back to the people. We were there and helped about 120 passers-by create 5 real life drawings.

Street Delivery 2011 - Friday

The board is an A4 sheet and has 24 tiles mostly made by artists from Romania, with some random tourists or expats here and there. It was created on Friday, 10th of June 2011.
